Origin Story

Thanos was born on Titan (the sixth orbiting moon of Saturn) as the son of A'Lars and Sui-San.

An Eternal with Deviant genes, Thanos embodys a lethal combination of unmatched strength, genius-level intellect, and a grim determination to bring "stability" to the universe by eliminating half of its population.

His resume includes conquering planets, defeating gods, wiping out 50% of all living creatures, and reducing the Avengers (Earth's mightiest heroes) to a shattered remnant drowning in regret.
